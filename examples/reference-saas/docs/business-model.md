# Business Model

Beacon Metrics sells workspace subscriptions to small product and engineering
teams. Starter costs USD 49 per workspace per month and supports three seats.
Growth costs USD 149 and supports fifteen seats. Annual billing is ten times the
monthly price.

Every workspace receives a 14-day trial. An activated workspace has completed a
successful source synchronization and saved its first dashboard. Paid
conversion is paid workspaces divided by trials that ended in the same cohort.
MRR includes active monthly subscription value plus one twelfth of active annual
contract value. Logo churn is canceled paying workspaces divided by paying
workspaces at the start of the month.

Pricing is workspace-based. Event volume is protected by fair-use limits but is
not an invoice dimension. Any packaging proposal must estimate effects on trial
activation, paid conversion, and logo churn.
