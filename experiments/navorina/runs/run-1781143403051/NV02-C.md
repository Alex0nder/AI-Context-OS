# NV02 (C)

## Answer

The factors that affect Navorina revenue most include:

- **Monetization Contour**: Different pricing tiers (free, plus, pro, premium, unlimited).
- **Integrations**: Dependencies on external services like Stripe, Resend, Push, Crypto PSP, and Xero.
- **Business Axes**: The effectiveness of the four business axes: Receive, Send, Account, and Deliver.
- **User Engagement**: Metrics such as assistant engagement, usage of Nucleus, and conversion rates of tools.
- **Non-goals**: Features that do not contribute to revenue, such as custody and certain integrations.

For a detailed understanding, refer to the business-core documentation and the PRD (Product Requirements Document).
