# NV22 (B)

## Answer

The increase in user churn may be attributed to the following factors:

- **Paywall Gates**: Users may be deterred by the paywall prompts, leading to higher churn rates.
- **AI Limits**: Restrictions on AI features could frustrate users, causing them to leave.
- **Integration Gaps**: Lack of live keys for integrations may limit functionality, impacting user satisfaction.
- **Generous Free Tier**: The free tier may be too generous, leading users to remain on it without converting to paid plans.

These factors can contribute to a negative user experience, ultimately resulting in increased churn.
