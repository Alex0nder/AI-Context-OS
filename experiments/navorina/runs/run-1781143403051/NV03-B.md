# NV03 (B)

## Answer

Monetization in Navorina works through the following mechanisms:

- **Subscription Tiers**:
  - **Free**: Basic access with limited features.
  - **Plus**: Includes snapshots, brief, export, and periodic review.
  - **Pro**: Full AI crew, invoice email, and acceptance of crypto payments.

- **Revenue Streams**:
  - Stripe subscriptions for Plus and Pro tiers.
  - Pro-only feature: crypto invoicing.

- **Key Features**:
  - Paywall prompts on surfaces like `subscribe.html` and settings for plan upgrades.
  - Event tracking and analytics using Amplitude to monitor user engagement and conversion metrics.

- **Metrics**:
  - Focus on conversion rates from free to paid subscriptions, retention rates, and engagement with assistant features.

- **Churn Drivers**:
  - Factors affecting user retention include paywall gates, AI limits, and integration gaps.

- **Analytics**:
  - Utilizes Amplitude for tracking user behavior and revenue-related metrics.

- **Risk Management**:
  - Identifies potential risks such as lack of a unified revenue dashboard and issues with the free tier being too generous.

This structure allows Navorina to generate revenue while providing different levels of service to users based on their subscription tier.
