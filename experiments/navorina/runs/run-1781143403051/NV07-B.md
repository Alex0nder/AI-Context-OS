# NV07 (B)

## Answer

- **Navorina**: A Crypto / Fin Hub that combines an assistant, tools, and a Nucleus workspace for freelancers and small teams, focusing on invoicing, crypto transactions, and financial management.

- **Product Core**:
  - **Purpose**: Defines the product context, including modules, pages, user flows, UX frameworks, and glossary.
  - **Key Components**:
    - **Assistant**: Chat interface with a 3D Sphere and voice capabilities.
    - **Nucleus**: Financial Mirror workspace for managing finances.
    - **Modules**: Marketing, assistant, App Suite, Nucleus FM, tools, and account management.
    - **UX Principles**: Calm fintech, progressive disclosure, and avoidance of anti-patterns.
    - **Key Files**: Includes HTML entries for Nucleus and Assistant, financial mirror core client, and various utility scripts.
  - **Key Entities**: Financial Mirror, Magic Inbox, Invoicing, Payments, Accounting, Insights, Tools, and Settings.
