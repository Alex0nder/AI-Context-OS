# NV01 (A)

## Answer

To determine why MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) dropped this month, the following context is needed:

- Specific data on customer churn rates or cancellations.
- Information on any changes in pricing or subscription tiers.
- Insights into customer feedback or issues that may have led to cancellations.
- Details on any new competitors or market changes affecting customer retention.
- Analysis of any changes in marketing or sales strategies that could impact revenue.

Without this information, it is not possible to accurately assess the reasons for the drop in MRR.
