# NV10 (A)

## Answer

To improve onboarding activation for the Navorina project, consider the following strategies:

- **Enhanced Wizard Experience**: 
  - Implement a more engaging onboarding wizard that guides users through key features and functionalities.
  - Allow users to choose between mandatory and skippable steps for personalization.

- **First Snapshot and Verdict**: 
  - Streamline the process for users to take their first snapshot and receive a verdict, making it intuitive and quick.

- **Aha Moment**: 
  - Ensure that users experience at least one significant insight or "aha" moment during onboarding to demonstrate value.

- **Assistant Integration**: 
  - Embed the assistant contextually within the onboarding process, providing prompts and assistance relevant to the user's current step.

- **Retention Nudges**: 
  - Use retention nudges, such as reminders or prompts, to encourage users to return and complete their onboarding tasks.

- **Feedback Mechanism**: 
  - Incorporate a feedback mechanism to gather user insights on the onboarding process, allowing for continuous improvement.

- **Email Follow-ups**: 
  - Send follow-up emails with tips and resources to help users who may not have completed the onboarding process.

These strategies aim to create a more engaging and supportive onboarding experience, ultimately leading to higher activation rates.
