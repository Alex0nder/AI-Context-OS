# NV41 (A)

## Answer

The CP8 crypto compliance in Settings refers to the following:

- **Compliance Settings**: It includes AML (Anti-Money Laundering) and partner compliance information.
- **User Interface**: Users can find compliance links and settings related to crypto payments.
- **Functionality**: It allows users to manage compliance requirements and view necessary disclaimers related to crypto transactions.
- **Location**: This feature is accessible under the Settings menu, specifically in the Crypto payments section.

For more detailed information, refer to the document section on crypto payments compliance.
