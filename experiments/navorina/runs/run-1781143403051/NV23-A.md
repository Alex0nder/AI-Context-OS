# NV23 (A)

## Answer

Navorina has the following retention levers:

- **Monthly Close**: 
  - Trigger: End of the month/checklist.
  - Action: Snapshot + verdict + brief.
  - Metric: Snapshots per month, D7 engagement.

- **Tasks Inbox**: 
  - Trigger: Overdue invoice/bill.
  - Action: Open Tasks → resolve.
  - Metric: Task resolution rate.

- **Glance**: 
  - Trigger: Push/email (later).
  - Action: Weekly brief (60s read).
  - Metric: Brief open rate.

- **Streak**: 
  - Trigger: Three consecutive weeks of data.
  - Action: Health score delta.
  - Metric: Weekly Active Users (WAU) in Nucleus.

- **Export**: 
  - Trigger: Accountant/tax season.
  - Action: Accounting package export.
  - Metric: Export events.
