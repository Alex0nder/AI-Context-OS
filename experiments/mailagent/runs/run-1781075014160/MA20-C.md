# MA20 (C)

## Answer

The plan limits are as follows:

- **Free Plan:**
  - Rate Limit: 60 requests per minute
  - Max Active Inboxes: 10
  - Max Team Keys: 5
  - Max Custom Domains: 1
  - Dedicated Resend: No

- **Pro Plan:**
  - Rate Limit: 300 requests per minute
  - Max Active Inboxes: 100
  - Max Team Keys: 20
  - Max Custom Domains: 10
  - Dedicated Resend: No

- **Enterprise Plan:**
  - Rate Limit: 600 requests per minute
  - Max Active Inboxes: 500
  - Max Team Keys: 50
  - Max Custom Domains: 25
  - Dedicated Resend: Yes

- **Legacy Plan:**
  - Rate Limit: 120 requests per minute
  - Max Active Inboxes: 500
  - Max Team Keys: 0
  - Max Custom Domains: 3
  - Dedicated Resend: No
