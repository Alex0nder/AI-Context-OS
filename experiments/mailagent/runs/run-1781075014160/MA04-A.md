# MA04 (A)

## Answer

MailAgent is a service that provides temporary email inboxes for AI agents and quality assurance (QA) or end-to-end (E2E) testing. It facilitates the verification of signups through methods like one-time passwords (OTP) and magic links, allowing automated processes without manual checks.

### Target Users:
- **AI Agents**: For integrating email verification into workflows.
- **QA/E2E Testers**: To automate testing scenarios involving email interactions, such as signup flows and verification processes.
