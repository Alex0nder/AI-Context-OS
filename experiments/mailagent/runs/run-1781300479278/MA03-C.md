# MA03 (C)

## Answer

The context does not provide specific reasons for users not receiving emails in their MailAgent inbox. However, potential issues could include:

- **Sender Not Allowed**: The sender's email may not be in the allowed senders list for the inbox.
- **Inbox Not Created**: The inbox may not have been created successfully or may have expired.
- **Domain Issues**: The domain associated with the inbox may not be verified or may not exist.
- **Callback URL Issues**: If a callback URL is set, it may not be functioning correctly.
- **Rate Limiting**: The inbox may be subject to rate limiting, preventing email delivery.
- **Configuration Errors**: There may be misconfigurations in the inbox settings or environment variables.

For a more accurate diagnosis, additional information about the specific error messages or logs would be needed.
