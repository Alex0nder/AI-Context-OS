## Answer

The plan limits for MailAgent are as follows:

| Plan        | `rateLimitPerMinute` | `maxActiveInboxes` | `maxTeamKeys` | `maxCustomDomains` | `dedicatedResend` |
|-------------|----------------------|--------------------|----------------|--------------------|-------------------|
| **free**    | 60                   | 10                 | 5              | 1                  | no                |
| **pro**     | 300                  | 100                | 20             | 10                 | no                |
| **enterprise** | 600                | 500                | 50             | 25                 | yes               |
| **legacy**  | 120                  | 500                | 0              | 3                  | no                |
