# MA04 (C)

## Answer

MailAgent is a service that provides temporary inboxes for AI agents and QA/E2E testing. It is designed to facilitate the verification of signups through methods like OTP (One-Time Password) and magic links, without the need for human intervention. 

### Target Users:
- **AI Developers**: For integrating and testing AI agents that require email verification.
- **QA Engineers**: For automating end-to-end tests that involve email interactions.
- **Developers**: Who need a solution for testing email functionalities in their applications without manual checks.
