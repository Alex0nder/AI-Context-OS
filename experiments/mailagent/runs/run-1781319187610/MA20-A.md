# MA20 (A)

## Answer

The plan limits for MailAgent are as follows:

| Plan       | Rate Limit (requests/min) | Active Inboxes |
|------------|---------------------------|-----------------|
| Free       | 60                        | 10              |
| Pro        | 300                       | 100             |
| Enterprise  | 600                       | 500             |
| Legacy     | 120                       | 500 (only for legacy `API_KEY`) |
