# MA03 (C)

## Answer

The context does not provide specific reasons for users not receiving emails in their MailAgent inbox. However, potential issues could include:

- **Domain Verification**: The inbox may be associated with a domain that is not verified.
- **Allowed Senders**: The sender's email address may not be in the allowed senders list for the inbox.
- **Inbox Expiration**: The inbox may have expired if the TTL (time-to-live) has elapsed.
- **Webhook Configuration**: The webhook for receiving emails may not be correctly set up or may be failing.
- **Resend Service Issues**: There could be issues with the Resend service that handles incoming emails.
- **Network or Configuration Errors**: There may be network issues or misconfigurations in the MailAgent setup.

For a definitive diagnosis, additional information about the specific inbox configuration and error logs would be needed.
