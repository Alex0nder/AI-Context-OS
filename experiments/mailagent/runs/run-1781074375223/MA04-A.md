# MA04 (A)

## Answer

MailAgent is a service that provides temporary inboxes for AI agents and quality assurance (QA) or end-to-end (E2E) testing. It facilitates the verification of email signups through mechanisms like one-time passwords (OTP) and magic links.

### Target Users:
- **AI Agents**: For automating email verification processes during signup flows.
- **QA/E2E Testers**: For testing applications that require email verification without manual checks or shared mailboxes.
